About TrekMate

Making trekking management simple, secure and accessible.

Who We Are

TrekMate is a web-based Trekking Management System designed to simplify and streamline the management of trekking activities. The platform provides a centralized solution where administrators can create and manage trekking routes, assign staff members, monitor bookings, and oversee user activities efficiently. Users can easily browse available treks, make online bookings, and track their booking history through an intuitive and user-friendly interface.

Built using Python, Flask, SQLite, SQLAlchemy, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and Jinja2, TrekMate offers a secure, responsive, and efficient web application for trekking management. The system implements role-based access control, secure user authentication, staff assignment, and an organized booking workflow, making it a reliable solution for both trekking organizers and adventure enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To provide an efficient and easy-to-use trekking management platform that simplifies trek planning, booking and administration for everyone.

Our Vision

To encourage outdoor adventure by using technology to make trekking safer, more organized and easily accessible for all trekking enthusiasts.
